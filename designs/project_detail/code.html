What does it do?

Neural Architect automates high-fidelity 3D modeling from spatial sketches, utilizing generative adversarial networks to predict structural integrity and lighting paths in real-time.

The Journey

What started as a simple mesh-deformation tool during Hackathon '23 evolved into a full-scale AI engine after integrating custom PyTorch modules for spatial reasoning.

The Idea

To bridge the gap between human intuition and complex CAD precision, allowing creators to "draw" buildings in virtual space.
